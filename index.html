What is Dancing?

Dancing is a form of art and movement, a lot of people use dancing to express emotions and how they feel. Dance is important as it builds strength and muscles and increases flexibility.

Types of Dancing

Some of the most important and popular styles of dancing are the following:

  • Ballet/Pointe
  • Contempary and Lyrical
  • Jazz
  • Hip-Hop
  • Tap Dancing
  • Acorbatics
  • Cheerleading/Poms
